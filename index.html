Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center Dallas Master Plan

The City of Dallas and project partners are working together to reimagine the Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center Dallas (KBHCCD) — transforming it into a world-class destination that connects Downtown Dallas, The Cedars, and the rest of the region.

Master Plan Components

Convention Center Expansion

Adding over 500,000 sq ft of modern event space and state-of-the-art facilities to elevate Dallas as a premier convention destination.

Dallas Memorial Auditorium

Preserving the historic auditorium while upgrading acoustics, lighting, and accessibility for modern performances.

The Black Academy of Arts & Letters (TBAAL)

Expanding and modernizing TBAAL to support arts education and cultural programming for the Dallas community.

Pioneer Plaza / Public Realm

Creating over 50 acres of parks and green spaces to connect downtown with surrounding neighborhoods and enhance public access.
